# Best Practices
## Order for Tailwind
1. Layout & Position: container, flex, grid, absolute, relative, inset, top, right, bottom, left. 
2. Box Model: margin (clockwise: mt, mr, mb, ml), padding, border, outline. 
3. Dimensions: width, height, min-width, min-height, max-width, max-height. 
4. Display: flex, grid, hidden. 
5. Colors & Backgrounds: bg-*, text-*, border-*, box-shadow, drop-shadow.
6. Typography: text-*, font-*, leading-*, tracking-*.
7. Effects & Interactions: transition, transform, hover:, focus:, animation, filter.
